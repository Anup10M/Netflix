Avengers: Endgame follows the aftermath of Infinity War, where Thanos wipes out half of all life using the Infinity Stones. The remaining Avengers, devastated by their loss, devise a plan to undo the destruction. Five years later, Ant-Man returns from the Quantum Realm and suggests time travel to retrieve the Infinity Stones before Thanos can use them. Splitting into teams, they revisit key moments in the past to collect the Stones. After successfully assembling the Infinity Gauntlet, Hulk reverses the Snap. However, a past version of Thanos travels to the present, leading to a climactic battle. Iron Man sacrifices himself to defeat Thanos and his army. With the universe restored, Captain America returns the Stones and chooses to live a full life in the past, passing his shield to Falcon. The film ends with the Avengers mourning their losses while looking toward the future.
