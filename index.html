Avengers Endgame


Avengers: Endgame is a 2019 superhero film and the epic conclusion to the Infinity Saga in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Directed by the Russo brothers, the movie follows the Avengers as they attempt to undo the devastating effects of Thanos' snap, which wiped out half of all life in the universe. With time travel, emotional sacrifices, and an epic final battle, the film delivers a thrilling mix of action, drama, and nostalgia.
